Bystranda is a Blue Flag beach in the southeastern part of the town center of Kristiansand , Norway . The shallow sandy beach by the Kristiansand Boardwalk consists of fine filled sand . By Bystranda there are seating areas , bathrobes stairs , sand volleyball court , skate ramp , playground and restrooms . At the plant of the bathing beach was emphasized that it should be accessible to children and the disabled . Bystranda is popular on hot summer days .
