Gemini Syndrome is a heavy metal band formed in 2010 in Los Angeles , California . The band currently consists of former Otep touring guitarist Aaron Nordstrom , guitarists Rich Juzwick and Mike Salerno , percussionist Steele Medina and bassist Alessandro Paveri . Their debut album ' ' Lux ' ' , was released in 2013 , drawing comparisons to bands like Disturbed , Stone Sour , and System of a Down . # Studio albums # # References #
