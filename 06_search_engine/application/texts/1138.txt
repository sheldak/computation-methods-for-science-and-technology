I Wonder If They Ever Think of Me is a song written and recorded by the American country music artist Merle Haggard . It was released in December 1972 as the first single from the album ' ' I Love Dixie Blues ' ' . I Wonder If They Ever Think of Me was Haggard 's fourteenth number one on the U.S. country singles chart .
