Dalum IF is a Danish association football club currently playing in the Denmark Series pool 2 . They play at Dalum Stadion in the southern Odense on Funen , which has a capacity of 4,000 . Founded in 1931 , the club rose to prominence in the late 1990s , playing two seasons in the second tier Danish 1st Division and six seasons in the third tier Danish 2nd Division from 1998 to 2006 . Starting from the 2006-07 season , they merged with B 1909 and B 1913 , with new team FC Fyn their combined first team . Dalum IF was named the reserve team of FC Fyn . As such , they must stay at least two divisions lower than FC Fyn.
