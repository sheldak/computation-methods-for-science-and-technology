birthplace = Cordenons , Italy Elio De Anna ( born September 30 , 1949 in Cordenons ) is an Italian former rugby union player turned politician , currently president of the Province of Pordenone ( Friuli-Venezia Giulia ) . As rugby player , De Anna twice won the Italian title with Rugby Rovigo , where he played alongside his brother Dino . He received 27 caps for the Italian team from 1972 to 1980 . He graduated in medicine , and in 2004 he was elected as president of the province of Pordenone for Forza Italia , as leader of centre-right coalition . After retiring as a player he practiced as a doctor , first in Rovigo then Cordenons , among the leadership positions held , including that of director of the Italian Athletics Federation . # Notes #
