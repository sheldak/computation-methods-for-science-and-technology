Ambt Montfort ( ) is a former municipality in the Dutch province of Limburg . In 1991 , the municipalities of Montfort , Posterholt , Reutje and Sint Odilinberg merged . The original name of the merged municipality was Posterholt , but it was changed in Ambt Montfort in 1994 . On January 1 , 2007 , Ambt Montfort merged into the municipality of Roerdalen.
