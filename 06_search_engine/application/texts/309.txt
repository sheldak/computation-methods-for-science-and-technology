Jack Oosterlaak ( ' ' Justus Kinloch Ayres Oosterlaak ; ' ' 15 January 1896 5 June 1968 ) , was a South African athlete who competed mainly in the 400 metres . He competed for South Africa in the 1920 Summer Olympics held in Antwerp , Belgium in the 4 x 400 metre relay where he won the Silver medal with his team mates Harry Davel , Clarence Oldfield and Bevil Rudd .
