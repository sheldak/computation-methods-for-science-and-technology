Zianchurinsky District ( ; ) is an administrative and municipal district ( raion ) , one of the fifty-four in the Republic of Bashkortostan , Russia . It is located in the south of the republic and borders with Kugarchinsky District in the north , Zilairsky District in the northeast and east , Khaybullinsky District in the east , and with Orenburg Oblast in the south and west . The area of the district is 3342.35 km2 . Its administrative center is the rural locality ( a ' ' selo ' ' ) of Isyangulovo . Population : 30,091 ( 2002 Census ) ; The population of Isyangulovo accounts for 26.9% of the district 's total population . # History # The district was established in 1930.
