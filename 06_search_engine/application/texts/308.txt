George Henry Goulding ( November 19 , 1884 &amp;ndash ; January 31 , 1966 ) was a Canadian athlete who competed mainly in the 10 kilometre walk . He was born in Hull , England . He competed in three events at the 1908 Olympic Games in London : the 3500 metre walking race , in which he came fourth ; the 10 mile walk , which he failed to finish ; and the Marathon , in which he came 22nd out of 55 starters and 27 finishers . The two walking distances were discontinued after London . He also competed for Canada in the 1912 Summer Olympics held in Stockholm , Sweden in the inaugural 10 kilometre walk where he won the Gold medal .
