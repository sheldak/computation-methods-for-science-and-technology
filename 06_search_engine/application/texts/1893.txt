Gamalakhe United FC are a South African football ( soccer ) club based in Gamalakhe , Port Shepstone that participates in the Vodacom League . The club was formed in 1986 and plays currently at Ugu Sports and Leisure Centre . The team has been a mainstay in the community for almost 30 years and started playing in the SAFA Second Division for four seasons . # Notable former players # * Ayanda Gcaba * Thamsanqa Teyise
