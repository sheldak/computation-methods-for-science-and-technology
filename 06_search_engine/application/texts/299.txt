Monterrubio de la Sierra is a village and municipality in the province of Salamanca , western Spain , part of the autonomous community of Castile-Leon . It is located 27 kilometres from the provincial capital city of Salamanca and has a population of 157 people . # Geography # The municipality covers an area of 35.31 km .
