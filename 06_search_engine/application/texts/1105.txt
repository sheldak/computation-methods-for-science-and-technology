subdivisiontype1 = State Bekwarra is a Local Government Area located in northern Cross River State , Nigeria . Its headquarters are in the town of Abuochiche ( or Abuo Chiche ) on the A4 highway . Other towns in the LGA are Nyanya-Ulim , Nyanya-Idigwe , Gakem , Itekpa , Ugboro , Ibiragidi , Utukwe , Afrike and Ukpa . It has an area of 306 km and a population of 105,822 at the 2006 census . The postal code of the area is 550.
