The Casino Internacional Tibidabo was a resort created in 1909 in Barcelona on the Tibidabo mountain . The building had a gaming room , theatre and bar . The games of chance offered were klondyke roulette and baccarat . Although gambling was forbidden by law in Spain , the casino succeeded in being operative during three years until the government closed it down in 1912 . Nowadays , the building is part of an amusement park . The automatons museum occupies the premises of the old theatre .
