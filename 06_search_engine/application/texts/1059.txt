Greg Kent was a player in the American Football League . He was also a player in the National Football League for the Oakland Raiders and Detroit Lions in 1966 and 1968 as a tackle and defensive end . He played at the collegiate level at the University of Utah and the University of WisconsinMadison. # Biography # Kent was born Edward Greg Kent on July 18 , 1943 in Elkhorn , Wisconsin . Kent is the co-owner of California Custom Carpets in Dublin , California .
