deathplace = Valley Forge , Pennsylvania Peter J. Camiel is a former Democratic member of the Pennsylvania State Senate , serving from 1953 to 1964 . He served as Chairman of the Philadelphia Democratic Party from 1969 , until 1976 , when he lost a power struggle with Mayor Frank Rizzo . He and three other defendants , including Vince Fumo and Thomas Nolan , were convicted of placing ghost workers on state payroll . The charges were later thrown out . A rest area on the Pennsylvania Turnpike is named in his honor .
