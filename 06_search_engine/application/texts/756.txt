image = website = Morn Airport ( ) is located 3 km southwest of the center of Morn , a city in the Buenos Aires Province of Argentina . The airport covers an area of 439 hectares ( 1,098 acres ) . It is located about 35 km from Buenos Aires city and was the main airport in Argentina prior to the opening of Ministro Pistarini International Airport in 1944 . Located here is the Museo Nacional de Aeronutica , an aircraft museum with historic aircraft on display .
