Shamweel Qasim ( sometimes rendered Shamweel ) is a Maldivian footballer nicknamed Bonda , who plays as midfielder for New Radiant SC . He is a member of the Maldives national football team . He is from the island of Hithadhoo , Addu Atoll . Shamweel is an important player for Maldives . # Personal life # Shamweel is married and he is a father of two .
