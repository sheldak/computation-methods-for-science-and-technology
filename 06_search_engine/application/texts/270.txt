Safarali Kenjayev ( b.1942 - died on 30 March 1999 ) served as the Speaker of the Supreme Soviet in Tajikistan 1991&amp;ndash ; 1992 , Chairman of the Tajik Parliament 's committee on human rights and legislation , and as head of Tajikistan 's Socialist Party which he founded . Kenjayev was a member of the Yaghnobi people In May 1992 protestors demonstrated outside the legislature building in Dushanbe , calling for Kenjayev 's dismissal because of his alleged corruption and mismanagement . As the civil war broke out Kenjayev and other leaders formed the Popular Front . On 24 October 1992 Kenjayev led his army into Dushanbe , attacking the capital 's government building . More than 150 people died . His forces , in control of the local radio station , proclaimed him the new president . Three assassins killed Kenjayev , his bodyguard , and his driver in Dushanbe on March 30 , 1999.
