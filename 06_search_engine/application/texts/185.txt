Westerland is a town in the Dutch province of North Holland . It is a part of the municipality of Hollands Kroon , and lies about 13 km southeast of Den Helder . In 2001 , the town of Westerland had 271 inhabitants . The built-up area of the town was 0.06 km , and contained 112 residences . The wider statistical area of Westerland has a population of around 750 . This includes the hamlet of Dam .
