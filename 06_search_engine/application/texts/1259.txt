James K. Baker is the founder of Dragon Systems . He is credited with creation of Dragon NaturallySpeaking . James Baker is an expert in speech recognition technology and a Distinguished Career Professor at Carnegie Mellon University . In June 2007 , Baker was appointed Director of Research at the new Human Language Technology Center of Excellence at the Johns Hopkins University . He recently coauthored a historical review on speech recognition with Xuedong Huang and Raj Reddy for CACM . Baker is a member of the United States National Academy of Engineering . # Publications # #
