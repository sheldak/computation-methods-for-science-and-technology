Alexandros Karachalios ( ; born 20 October 1985 ) is a Greek footballer currently playing for Kalamata F.C . in the Football League 2 ( Greece ) as a right Winger . # Career # Born in Athens , Karachalios began his playing career with Panionios and has played in the Greek Super League for Panionios and Akratitos.
