' ' Coleophora spumosella ' ' is a moth of the Coleophoridae family . It has a disjunct distribution and is found in France , Switzerland , Spain , Italy , Croatia and southern Russia . The larvae feed on ' ' Astragalus ' ' , ' ' Dorycnium pentaphyllum ' ' , ' ' Medicago ' ' and ' ' Ononis ' ' species . They create a black pistol case with a large transparent-white pallium that leaves a small part of the case uncovered . The mouth angle is about 45 . Larvae can be found from autumn to April .
