' ' Ventilago ' ' is a genus of plants in the family Rhamnaceae . It includes about 35 species found in the tropics of Australasia , with one species each in Africa and Madagascar . The roots of ' ' Ventilago neo-caledonica ' ' are used in Vanuatu to produce a crimson red dye ( known in some local languages as ' ' labwa ' ' or ' ' labwe ' ' ) which is used to pattern traditional textiles .
