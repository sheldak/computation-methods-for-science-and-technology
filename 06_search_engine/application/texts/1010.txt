Dance Little Dreamer is a 1978 disco single by Bionic Boogie , a group created by disco producer , Gregg Diamond and was included in Bionic Boogie 's , self-titled , 1978 album . Dance Little Dreamer , along with the track , Risky Changes made it to number on the disco charts for three weeks . Despite the popularity of the album cuts , both failed to make both the soul and pop singles chart .
