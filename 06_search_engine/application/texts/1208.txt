Gergely Palgyi ( born 19 February 1979 ) is a Hungarian hurdler who specializes in the 110 metres hurdles . He finished fifth at the 1998 World Junior Championships , and also competed at the 2002 European Indoor Championships , the 2004 World Indoor Championships and the 2005 Summer Universiade without reaching the final . He became Hungarian national champion in 1998 , interfering with a long winning streak by Levente Csillag , and Hungarian indoor champion in 2003 and 2005 . His personal best time is 13.82 seconds , achieved in June 2005 in Budapest . From March 2006 to March 2008 he was suspended by the IAAF for a doping-related offense.
