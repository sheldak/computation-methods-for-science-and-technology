birthplace = Lagos , Nigeria position = Striker John Olufemi Otuagomah Sfondo ( born 7 April 1984 in Lagos ) is a Nigerian footballer . He is currently a free agent . # Career # Olufemi was on loan to Rovigo from Palermo in summer 2006 , but in January 2007 , he left for Bellinzona . He then transferred to FC Locarno on February 2008 , in a deal effective as of summer 2008.
