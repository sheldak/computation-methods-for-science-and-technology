Suleyman al-Boustani ( Arabic : / ALA-LC : ' ' Sulaymn al-Bustn ' ' ; 18561925 ) was born in Bkheshtin , Lebanon . He was a statesman , teacher , poet and historian . He was a Maronite Catholic and hailed from a prominent family well known for their pioneering contributions to the Arab renaissance of the late 19th century known as the Nahda . A nephew of Butrus al-Bustani , he was famous for translating Homer 's Iliad into Arabic , introducing its poetic style into the Arabic language . His political front saw him as the minister of finance in the last Ottoman government before its collapse .
