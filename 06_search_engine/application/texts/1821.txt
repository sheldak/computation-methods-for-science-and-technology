Maxim Victorovich Demin is a Russian businessman . In 2011 , Demin became co-owner of English Football League side A.F.C. Bournemouth with Eddie Mitchell , reportedly paying 850,000 for his share of the club . Demin has been involved in the petrochemicals industry and as a trader . # Property # Maxim Demin spent 5 million on a mansion located in Sandbanks ( Dorset ) , which he then demolished and employed Eddie Mitchell to rebuild a state of the art house .
