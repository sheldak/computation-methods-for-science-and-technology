Kasparov is a Russian surname People with this surname include : * Andrey Kasparov ( born 1966 ) , Armenian-American pianist , composer and professor . * Garry Kasparov ( born 1963 ) , Russian former World Chess Champion . * Gevorg Kasparov ( born 1980 ) , Armenian football goalkeeper . * Yuri Kasparov ( born 1955 ) , Russian composer . Branded products include * Kasparov 's Gambit , a 1993 chess video game by Electronic Arts * Virtual Kasparov , a chess video game Kasparov is often confused with another Russian surname : * Gasparov
