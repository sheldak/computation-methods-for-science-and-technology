**26;268104;TOOLONG The Mobarak Mosque in The Hague is the first purpose-built mosque in the Netherlands . The Ahmadiyya Muslim Community came to the Netherlands in 1947 and Qudrat-Ullah Hafiz was the first missionary . The mosque designed by Frits Beck was opened by Sir Muhammad Zafarullah Khan on 9th of December 1955 . On 3 June 2006 Queen Beatrix of the Netherlands had visited the Mobarak Mosque to commemorate the building 's 50th anniversary .
