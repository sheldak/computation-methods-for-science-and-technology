Schmidt Peninsula ( ) is a small peninsula connected by a low isthmus to Cape Legoupil , Trinity Peninsula . Its western extremity is Bahamonde Point . The peninsula was named by the Chilean Antarctic Expedition of 1947-48 for Captain Hugo Schmidt Prado , Chilean Army , the first commander of Base Bernardo O'Higgins established in 1948 on this peninsula . # Map # * Scale 1:250000 topographic map No. 5697 .
