Judith van Kampen ( born December 21 , 1978 in Amsterdam ) is a Dutch softball player , who represents the Dutch national team in international competitions . Van Kampen played for Pioniers , HCAW , University of Nevada , Reno , Terrasvogels and since 2008 for Sparks Haarlem . She is a pitcher and throws right-handed . She competes for the Dutch national team since 2001 . She is part of the Dutch team for the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing . Her brother Michiel van Kampen is baseballer and also represented the Netherlands at the same Olympics .
