Heyran ( ) is a set of three villages ( Heyran-e Olya , Heyran-e Vosta , and Heyran-e Sofla ; Upper- , Middle- , and Lower-Heyran respectively ) in the northwest of Astara . The villages are located at a distance of 30 km from Astara County by the road from Astara to Ardabil . Winters are cold and snowy ; summers are cool . Because of having a nice climate at summer time , many villas are built in the neighborhood area . The main road is totally overcrowded at weekends . There is also a Gondola lift at higher altitudes of village . Heyran is one of the most famous touristic sites in Iran regarding its beautiful and unique scenery .
