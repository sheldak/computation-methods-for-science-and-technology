The Battle of El Nmero , was a major battle during the years after the Dominican War of Independence and was fought on April 19 , 1849 , nearby Azua de Compostela , Azua Province . A force of Dominican troops , a portion of the Army of The South , led by General Pedro Santana , encountered an outnumbering force of 10,000 troops of the Haitian Army led by General Jean Francois Jeannot.
