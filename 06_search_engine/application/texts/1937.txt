' ' Trischalis subaurana ' ' is a moth in the Arctiidae family . It was described by Walker in 1863 . It is found in the Indo-Australian tropics , from the north-eastern Himalayas and China ( Hainan ) to the Bismarck Archipelago . The habitat consists of forests , ranging from the lowlands to the lower montane zone at about 1,000 meters.
