( ) Sri Medananda Daham Pasala ( ) is a school located in the Akuressa Matara Road Near the Palatuwa Village . It was initially founded as a Daham school in 1974 . The founder of the school was the Rer . Baragama Dammaloka Thero incumbent of Unella Sri Nagarama Purana Viharaya .
