Siwiaka is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Starogard Gdaski , within Starogard County , Pomeranian Voivodeship , in northern Poland . It lies approximately 13km mi north of Starogard Gdaski and 33kmmi 0 south of the regional capital Gdask . For details of the history of the region , see ' ' History of Pomerania ' ' . The village has a population of 263.
