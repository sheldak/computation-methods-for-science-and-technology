John Jack Kelly , born on 15 September 1930 at Conisbrough , Yorkshire , played first-class cricket for Nottinghamshire from 1953 to 1957 . He later played Minor Counties cricket for Devon . Kelly was a left-handed middle-order batsman and a slow left-arm orthodox spin bowler . In three seasons , 1953 , 1954 and 1956 , he played in around half of Nottinghamshire 's matches .
