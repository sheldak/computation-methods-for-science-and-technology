Modeling chocolate , also called chocolate leather , plastic chocolate or candy clay , is a chocolate paste made by melting chocolate and combining it with corn syrup , glucose syrup , or golden syrup . Primarily used by upscale cakemakers and ptisseries to add decoration to cakes and pastries , modeling chocolate is formed into a variety of shapes and structures that can not be easily accomplished with other softer edible materials such as buttercream frosting , marzipan , or fondant . Modeling chocolate can be made from white , dark , semi-sweet , or milk chocolate .
