Sigmund Freudenberger ( 16 June 1745 &amp;ndash ; 15 November 1801 ) was a Swiss painter . Freudenberger was the son of a lawyer , and studied as a portraitist . He lived from 1765 to 1773 in Paris , where he worked with Franois Boucher . He then founded in a private art school in Bern and was known as Little Master for his genre-like depictions of rural life .
