Kyyneleet is a song by Finnish rapper Cheek . The song features an appearance by a soul singer Sami Saari and a sample of Saari 's song Onnen kyyneleet . The song was released as a fifth and final single from Cheek 's seventh studio album ' ' Sokka irti ' ' . Kyyneleet peaked at number 20 on the Finnish Singles Chart . A music video , directed by Petri Lahtinen , was uploaded to YouTube in February 2013. # Charts #
