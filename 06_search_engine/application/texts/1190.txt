governingbody = Private Fisk Chapel ( also known as A.M.E. Bethel Chapel ) is a historic cruciform chapel on Cedar Avenue in Fair Haven , Monmouth County , New Jersey , United States . It was built in 1882 in the Carpenter Gothic style for the town 's African-American community and added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1975 , upon the congregation 's relocation to a newer building .
