' ' Setiostoma dietzi ' ' is a species of sedge moths in the genus ' ' Setiostoma ' ' . It was described by Duckworth in 1971 . It is found in the humid low highlands of western Venezuela , central Colombia and Costa Rica . The wingspan is 1115 mm . The forewings are yellow basally to just before the midpoint , the distal margin of the yellow area runs straight from the costa to the dorsum and is bordered by a dark brown line . The apical half of the forewing is dark brown with a broad transverse band parallel to the distal margin of the yellow area and a subterminal band from the costa before the apex to the tornus . There are irregular , iridescent blue to violet patches between two preceding fascia . The hindwings are dark brown with a white patch on the anterior margin .
