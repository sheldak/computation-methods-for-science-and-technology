Philharmonic Hall refers to multiple music venues : * Azerbaijan State Philharmonic Hall in Baku , Azerbaijan * Philharmonic Hall , Cologne below the level of the Roncalliplatz in Cologne is the Philharmonic Hall , with seating for 2,000 rising in concentric segments of a circle . * Philharmonic Hall , Liverpool * Avery Fisher Hall at the Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts , New York . Known as Philharmonic Hall from its opening in 1962 to its renaming for Avery Fisher in 1973
