Aodh mac Aidmhire , Irish dynast , fl. c. 600 . Aodh was the father of seven notable saints : Bairrfhionn ; Fionnbharr ; Maoldubh ; Crnn ; Gar Bhn ; Sabh ; Aille . Aodh was from Aughrim , County Galway , and is given as a descendant of Brin mac Echach Muigmedin . In the genealogies he is given as ' ' Aodh , from Eachdhruim mac nAodha. ' '
