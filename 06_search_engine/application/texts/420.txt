' ' Live In Gottingen ' ' is a live album by Psychic TV . The album was released only as a 12 vinyl in a limited edition of 5,000 copies although the performance would later be released on CD along with another performance as ' ' Mein-Goett-In-Gen ' ' . Recorded live in Gttingen , West Germany on May 17 , 1984 .
