Needmore is a census-designated place in southern Fulton County , Pennsylvania . It lies at the intersection of U.S. Route 522 ( Great Cove Road ) and Route 655 ( Thompson Road ) in Belfast Township . As of the 2010 census the population was 170 . Needmore was created on April 27 , 1954. # Geology # The Devonian geologic formation called the Needmore Formation is named after the town of Needmore.
