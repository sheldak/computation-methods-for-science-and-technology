Raymond de Preille ( Catalan : Ramon de Perella , Occitan : Raimon de Perelha ) was an Occitan nobleman who was the lord of Chteau de Montsgur . Born about 1186 , he rebuilt the destroyed castle after 1204 . He married in 1222 and resided over the castle together with his cousin Pierre-Roger de Mirepoix who also became his in-law by marrying Philippa , Raymond 's daughter , around 1239 . Under Raymond 's protection , Guilhabert de Castres set up the center of Catharism at Montsgur around 1232 . The fall of Montsgur in 1244 ended organized Carthar activity in the Occitan region . Surviving the end of Montsgur he was interrogated by the inquisition in May , 1244.
