Connecticut 's 140th House district is one of 151 Connecticut House of Representatives districts . It is currently represented by Bruce Morris . The district consists of the central part of the city of Norwalk. # List of representatives # Prior to redistricting in 1972 , Otha Brown , Jr . represented the 148th District and John A. Fabrizio represented the 147th . The redistricting formed the 140th from the old 148th , 147th and part of the 145th District . This forced a contest between the two incumbents .
