The Siamese mud carp ( ' ' Henicorhynchus siamensis ' ' ) is a species of freshwater cyprinid fish , a variety of Asian carp native to the Mekong and Chao Phraya Rivers in Southeast Asia , especially in Cambodia , Laos and Thailand . It is very present in floodplains during the wet season and migrates upstream in the Mekong starting in Cambodia . The Siamese mud carp has locally commercial use both as food and in the aquarium trade .
