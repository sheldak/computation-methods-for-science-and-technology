Julin Dobrski ( 31 December 1811 or 1812 , Nowe - 2 May 1886 , Warsaw ) was a Polish operatic tenor . He was one of the main tenors at the Great Theatre , Warsaw from 1832-1861 , and again in 1865 when he sang the role of Stefan in the world premiere of Stanisaw Moniuszko 's ' ' The Haunted Manor ' ' .
