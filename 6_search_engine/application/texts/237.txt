* ' ' Abia ' ' Leach , 1817 * ' ' Cimbex ' ' Olivier , 1790 * ' ' Corynis ' ' Thunberg , 1789 * ' ' Praia ' ' Wankowicz , 1880 * ' ' Pseudoclavellaria ' ' Schultz , 1906 * ' ' Trichiosoma ' ' Leach , 1817 The Cimbicidae are a small family of large-bodied , often hairy sawflies , with only 130 species in six genera worldwide . Larvae are solitary herbivores . The family is distinctive in having antennae with prominent apical clubs or knobs . The adults of some species can exceed 3 cm in length , and are among the heaviest of all Hymenoptera.
