Thomas J. MacDonald ( born 1966 ) is a U.S. carpenter and woodworker and host of the PBS television series ' ' Rough Cut Woodworking with Tommy Mac ' ' . Born in Dorchester , Massachusetts , MacDonald attended the Blue Hills Regional Vocational High School , but dropped out and did not complete his schooling ; he later received a GED . He worked as a carpenter for 17 years , but was injured while doing construction work on the Big Dig project in Boston . He later attended the North Bennet Street School in Boston , where he learned woodworking . He started his own business in Canton , Massachusetts , and his work was noticed by Bob Vila , who later invited him to appear on his television series ' ' Home Again ' ' and on video podcasts for the BobVila.com website.
