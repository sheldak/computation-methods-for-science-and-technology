' ' Sisterhood ' ' is a comedy feature film shot in London and Governors Bay , Christchurch , New Zealand and completed in 2008 . It premiered in London 's West End on 23 October 2008 , followed by a theatrical release in the UK . It played in competition at the inaugural The Feel Good Film Festival in Los Angeles between 2224 August . The cast includes Nicholas Ball , Maria Charles , Rory McGregor , Graham McTavish , Isabelle Defaut , Emily Corcoran , Robert Faith and Jon Gadsby . The film was directed by Richard Wellings-Thomas , written by Emily Corcoran and produced by Emily Corcoran , Tim Hart and Abi Sirokh.
