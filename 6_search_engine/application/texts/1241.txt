subdivisiontype1 = County Kru is a small borough ( ) in Rapla County , Estonia . It is the administrative centre of Kru Parish . Kru has a station on the Edelaraudtee 's western route . # Kru manor # Kru ( ) was established as an estate in the mid-18th century . The present building was built in 1878 and designed by Riga architect Robert Pflug . It is an eclectic building with mainly neo-Renaissance elements . It was damaged during the Revolution of 1905 and also during World War II . The manor house ensemble has several well-preserved and unusual outbuildings and annexes . Explorer Karl von Ditmar was the landowner of Kru .
