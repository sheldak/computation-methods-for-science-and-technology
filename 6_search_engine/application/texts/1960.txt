Martha Winch ( born 31 October 1978 in Sydney , New South Wales ) is an Australian former cricket player . Winch played domestic cricket for the New South Wales Breakers between 1997 and 2007 . She was a member of the Breakers team that won five consecutive Women 's National Cricket League titles between 2005/06 and 2009/10 , beginning with the 2005/06 finals series against the Queensland Fire . Winch played seven One Day Internationals for the Australia national women 's cricket team .
