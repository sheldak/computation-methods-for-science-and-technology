The Love Ride is a charity motorcycle ride held annually in Southern California and in Switzerland . Its organizers bill it as The Largest One-Day Motorcycle Fund-Raising Event in the World . # Harley-Davidson of Glendale , CA # It was founded by Oliver Shokouh , the owner of a Harley-Davidson dealership in Glendale , California . Comedian and ' ' The Tonight Show ' ' host Jay Leno has served as grand marshall every year since 1985 . Other celebrity participants have included Lorenzo Lamas and Larry Hagman , Robert Patrick , and musicians Lynyrd Skynyrd . The original ride in 1984 raised $1,500 .
