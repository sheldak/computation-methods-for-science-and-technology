' ' Udea micacea ' ' is a moth of the Crambidae family . It is endemic to Kauai , Oahu , Molokai and Maui . The color pattern in adult males differs from that of females . In the males there is a depressed , longitudinal , pale streak between veins 6 and 7 at the base . The larvae feed on ' ' Cyrtandra ' ' species , including ' ' Cyrtandra cordifolia ' ' .
