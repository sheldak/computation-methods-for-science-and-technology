subdivisiontype1 = Voivodeship Pasterka ( ) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Radkw , within Kodzko County , Lower Silesian Voivodeship , in south-western Poland . Prior to 1945 it was in Germany . It lies approximately 5km mi west of Radkw , 25km mi west of Kodzko , and 100km mi south-west of the regional capital Wrocaw . The village has a population of 50.
