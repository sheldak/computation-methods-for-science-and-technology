The Naggar School of Photography , Media and New Music ( better known as the Musrara School of Photography ) is an art school in the Musrara neighborhood of Jerusalem , Israel . The Naggar School of Photography , Media and New Music was founded in 1986 . Students can follow alternative forms of study . Graduates are required to complete an independent project . Many graduates have won awards ; graduates have displayed their work in galleries in Israel and in other countries .
