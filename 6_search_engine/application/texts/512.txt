Farm to Market Road 3405 ( FM 3405 ) is a 7.561mi km farm to market road in Williamson County , Texas . # Route description # FM 3405 begins at north of Liberty Hill . The route travels to the east , and the north fork of the San Gabriel River lies to its south . The highway ends at northwest of the city of Georgetown and Lake Georgetown. # History # FM 3405 was first designated along its current route in 1977. # Major intersections #
