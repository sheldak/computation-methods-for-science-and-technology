The Peru Cup 1967 season ( ) is the promotion tournament of Peruvian football . In this tournament after many qualification rounds , each one of the 24 political departments of Peru qualifies a team . Those teams , plus the team relegated from the First Division at the end of the previous season , play in two more rounds . Finally six of the clubs qualify for the Final round , staged in Lima ( the capital ) .
