Tim Maddison was a rugby league footballer of the ' 90s and ' 00s . He played for the Newcastle Knights from 1993 to 1995 and 2003 , then the Sydney City Roosters in 1996 , the Hunter Mariners in 1997 , the Cronulla-Sutherland Sharks from 1998 to 2000 and finally the North Queensland Cowboys from 2001 to 2002.
