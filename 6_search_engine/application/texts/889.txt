Erik Christian Larsen ( 20 February 1928 &amp;ndash ; 10 April 1952 ) was a Danish rower who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics . He was born in Herflge , Kge Municipality and died in Ringberg , Region Sjlland . In 1948 he was a crew member of the Danish boat which won the bronze medal in the coxed fours event .
