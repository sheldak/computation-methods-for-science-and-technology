Dick Wardill ( born 5 July 1872 ) was an Australian rules footballer who played with Melbourne in the Victorian Football League ( VFL ) . A ruckman , Wardill became Melbourne 's first ever premiership winning captain when he led his side to victory in the 1900 Grand Final against Fitzroy.
