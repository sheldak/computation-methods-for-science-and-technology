class = L1 callsignmeaning = KBDL-LP ( 107.9 FM , NOAA Weather Radio ) is a low-power FM radio station licensed to Carbondale , Colorado , USA . The station is currently owned by State of Colorado Telecom Services . # History # The Federal Communications Commission issued a construction permit for the station on October 26 , 2001 . The station was assigned the KBDL-LP call sign on January 14 , 2002 , and received its license to cover on October 4 , 2002.
