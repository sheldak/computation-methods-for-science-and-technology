subdivisiontype1 = Province Teminabuan is a small town in South Sorong Regency , West Papua , Indonesia . The town serves as the capital city of South Sorong Regency . As of 2005 it had a population of 7969 . The town is located in the southwestern-central part of the Bird 's Head Peninsula . The area was bombed between January and March 1967 . It is served by Teminabuan Airport .
