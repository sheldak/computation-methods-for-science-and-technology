Gierzwad ( German ' ' Geyerswalde ' ' ) is a village in Ostrda County , Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship , in northern Poland . It is the seat of the gmina ( administrative district ) called Gmina Grunwald . It lies approximately 19km mi south-east of Ostrda and 38kmmi 0 south-west of the regional capital Olsztyn . The village has a population of 638.
