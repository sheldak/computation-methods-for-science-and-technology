Saint-Men-le-Grand ( , Gallo : ' ' Saent-Men ' ' ) is a commune in the Ille-et-Vilaine department in Brittany in northwestern France . It is located southwest of Rennes between Montauban-de-Bretagne and Gal . At the 2006 Tour de France , Saint-Men-le-Grand hosted the start of Stage 8. # Population # Inhabitants of Saint-Men-le-Grand are called ' ' mvennais ' ' in French .
