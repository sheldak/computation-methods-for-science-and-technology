Stord Kisgruber was a Norwegian mining company established in 1907 . The company operated the pyrite mines at Litlab in Stord , Norway . The mining operations ended in 1968 . A total of about eight million tons of ore was won during the operation of the mine . A railway line operated between Litlab and the quays at Sagvg . During the German occupation of Norway , the mines delivered pyrite to Germany for use by the country 's war industry . In 1941 this amounted to 9,000 tons per month . In January 1943 the mines were targeted by the British Commando raid Operation Cartoon . A museum ( ) has later been established at Litlab , to commemorate the mines and the accompanying society .
