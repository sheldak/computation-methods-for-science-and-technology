John Fortune Jack Lawrence ( 9 September 1904 13 August 1984 ) was an Irish cricketer . # Biography # Jack Lawrence was born John Fortune Lawrence in Dublin , Ireland on 9 September 1904 . A right-handed batsman , he played one first-class cricket match for Dublin University against Northamptonshire in June 1926 . Lawrence died on 13 August 1984 , in Eastbourne , Sussex , England .
