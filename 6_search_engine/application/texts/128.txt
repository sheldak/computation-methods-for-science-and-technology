Eddie Lubanski ( born c. 1929 ) was one of the greatest bowlers of all time . He was in the Guinness book of World Records . He had the highest lifetime average of 204 for over 25 years . On June 22 , 1959 he bowled two consecutive 300 games . The event was televised from Miami and paired him with a female bowler . He bowled and if he did n't strike , she would attempt a spare pick-up . She would bowl until she missed a strike . Lubanski bowled strikes for two games and she never attempted a spare .
