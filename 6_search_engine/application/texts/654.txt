West Barsham is a village within the civil parish of Barsham which is in the English county of Norfolk . The village is one of four settlements within the parish of Barsham . The other villages are North Barsham , East Barsham and Houghton St Giles . West Barsham is 3.2 miles north of the town of Fakenham , 24.1 miles west of Cromer and 117 miles north of London . The nearest railway station is at Sheringham for the Bittern Line which runs between Sheringham , Cromer and Norwich . The nearest airport is Norwich International Airport .
