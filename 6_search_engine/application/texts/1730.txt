Nakuul Mehta ( born in Mumbai , India ) is an Indian actor and model , best known for his lead role as Aditya in the television series ' ' Pyaar Ka Dard Hai Meetha Meetha Pyaara Pyaara ' ' . He currently lives in Mumbai , Maharashtra . Mehta was born in Udaipur , Rajasthan , India . He descends from King Prithviraj Chauhan of Rajput Chauhan dynasty of Mewar . His father , Pratap Singh Mehta , is a veteran of Indo-Pakistani War of 1971 , while Mehta 's great great grandfather Laxmilal Mehta was the military-in-chief of the Mewar region . Mehta is a practising Buddhist .
