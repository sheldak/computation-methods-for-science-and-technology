Charlie Smith ( June 20 1956 8 November 2005 ) was Romnichal poet who had a booklet of his poems published in the 1980s , which included the acclaimed Space Gypsies poem . He was from a family of tailors , his schooling was not good and he left early , thinking himself semi-illiterate .
