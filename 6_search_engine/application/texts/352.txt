' ' Live and Sleazy ' ' is the fifth studio album by the Village People . It was released as a double-LP with studio and live recordings . The album featured numerous lead singers : the original cop Victor Willis ( tracks 1-6 ) , the current cop Ray Simpson ( tracks 8 , 9 and 11 ) , the construction worker David Hodo ( track 7 ) and the G.I.
