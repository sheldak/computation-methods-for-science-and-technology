Jamie Murch ( born 8 August 1975 ) was an Australian cricketer . He was a right-handed batsman and right-arm medium-fast bowler who played for Hertfordshire . He was born in Melbourne . Murch made a single appearance for the team , during the C&amp;G Trophy in August 2003 . From the middle order , he scored 24 runs with the bat , and took figures of 0-67 with the ball .
