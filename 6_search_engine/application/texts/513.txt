' ' Cordulegaster helladica ' ' is a species of dragonfly in family Cordulegastridae . It is endemic to Greece . Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical dry forests , rivers , and freshwater springs . It is threatened by habitat loss . # Sources # * Boudot , J.-P. &amp; Leipelt , K.G. 2005 . .
