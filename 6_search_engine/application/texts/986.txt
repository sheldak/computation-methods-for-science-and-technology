Gennaro Annese ( 1604 - June 20 , 1648 ) was an Italian revolutionary , who led the rebels in Naples against Spain in 1647-1648 . Annese was an arquebus maker who lived near the Porta of the Carmine . He succeeded Masaniello during the Neapolitan Revolt of 1647 . The following year , in April , the Spanish troops entered Naples and Annese surrendered after having been besieged in the Carmine Castle . In June , Annese was arrested and jailed in the Castel Nuovo ; after a short process , he was sentenced to death and executed in the same castle .
