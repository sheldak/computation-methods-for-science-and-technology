34088 Satokosuka ( provisional designation : 2000 PC <sub> 7 </sub> ) is a main-belt minor planet . It was discovered through the BATTeRS survey at the Bisei Spaceguard Center in Bisei , Okayama Prefecture , Japan , on August 6 , 2000 . It is named for Kosuke Sato , a Japanese elementary school student who won a 2008 astronomy-themed painting contest .
