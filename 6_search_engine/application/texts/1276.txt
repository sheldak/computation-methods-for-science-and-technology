' ' Salvia filicifolia ' ' is a perennial plant that is native to Guangdong and Hunan provinces in China , growing in rocky and sandy areas . ' ' S. filicifolia ' ' grows on erect or slightly ascending stems , with inflorescences that are 6-10 flowered verticillasters in pedunculate racemes or panicles , with a 8mm in yellow corolla . # Notes #
