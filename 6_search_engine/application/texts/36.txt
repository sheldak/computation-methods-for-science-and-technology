Esclados is a character in Arthurian romance , the guardian of an otherworldly fountain in the forest of Brocliande . He appears in Chrtien de Troyes ' medieval French poem ' ' Yvain , the Knight of the Lion ' ' as Esclados the Red ( ) , spouse of the lady Laudine ( usually known as the Lady of the Fountain ) prior to Sir Yvain . He was slain by Yvain for attacking the latter 's cousin , Sir Calogrenant , in defense of the magic fountain ( or well , or spring ) .
