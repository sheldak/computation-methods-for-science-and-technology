competitors = 12 The individual championship test , grade III , para-equestrian dressage event at the 2012 Summer Paralympics was contested on 2 September at Greenwich Park in London . The competition was assessed by a ground jury composed of five judges placed at locations designated E , H , C , M , and B. Each judge rated the competitors ' performances with a percentage score .
