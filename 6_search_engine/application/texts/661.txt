' ' Jewels of Gwahlur ' ' is a 1979 collection of two fantasy short stories written by Robert E. Howard featuring his sword and sorcery hero Conan the Barbarian . The book was published in 1979 by Donald M. Grant , Publisher , Inc. as volume VIII of their deluxe Conan set . The title story originally appeared in the magazine ' ' Weird Tales ' ' . The Snout in the Dark is the original fragment of a story that Howard never completed . It first appeared , completed by L. Sprague de Camp and Lin Carter , in the collection ' ' Conan of Cimmeria ' ' . # Contents # * Jewels of Gwahlur * The Snout in the Dark
