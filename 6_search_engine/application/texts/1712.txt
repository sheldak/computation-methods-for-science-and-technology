subdivisiontype1 = State Chilakamukhi also spelled as ' ' Chilkmukhi ' ' ( ) is a village in the Koppal taluk of Koppal district in the Indian state of Karnataka . Chilakamukhi is located northeast to District Headquarters Koppal. # Demographics # As of 2001 India census , Chilakamukhi had a population of 1,502 with 755 males and 747 females and 239 Households .
