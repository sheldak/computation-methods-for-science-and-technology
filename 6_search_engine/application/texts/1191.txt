: ' ' Not to be confused with the German philologist of the same name . ' ' Hans Christian Hagedorn ( 18881971 ) was the creator of NPH insulin and Nordisk Insulinlaboratorium . This is known today as Novo Nordisk. # Biography # Hagedorn and August Krogh ( 18741949 ) obtained the rights for insulin from Banting and Best in Toronto . In 1923 they formed Nordisk Insulinlaboratorium , and in 1926 with August Krogh he obtained a Danish Royal Charter as a non-profit foundation .
