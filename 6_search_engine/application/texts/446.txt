The Central Division of New South Wales is one of the three divisions of New South Wales along with the Eastern and Western divisions , established under the Crown Lands Act of 1884 for the purposes of land management . In 1907 , it included the Hay , Forbes , Dubbo and Moree land boards , as well as the western parts of the Wagga Wagga and Tamworth land boards . Part of its western boundary is the Lachlan River , and it includes a large part of the Riverina.
