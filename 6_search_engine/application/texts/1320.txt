Nasir ad-Din al-Qasri Muhammad ibn Ahmad , also Nasir al-Qasiri , was the young son of the Sultan of Fez , Sultan Ahmad . In 1545 , Sultan Ahmad was taken prisoner by his southern rivals the sharifian Sadiyans . His successor , Ali Abu Hassun , regent for Nasir al-Qasiri , decided to pledge allegiance to the Ottomans in order to obtain their support . He ruled with a regent from 1545 to 1547 during the imprisonment of his father by the Sadiyans. # Notes #
