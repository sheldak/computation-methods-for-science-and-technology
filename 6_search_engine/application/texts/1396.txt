Leonora Sparkes ( 1883- ? ) was the British mezzo-soprano for the Metropolitan Opera in New York City in the 1910s. # Biography # She was born in 1883 in England . Dippel heard her sing at Covent Garden and after a private concert offered her a contract at the Metropolitan Opera in 1908 . She appeared in the American premiere of ' ' Boris Godunov ' ' at the Metropolitan Opera as Tsarevna Xenia Borisovna of Russia on March 19 , 1913 with Arturo Toscanini conducting . She sang with Enrico Caruso three times , including in the MET 's production of Aida in 1913 . Her career was primarily spent in London and New York , but she also sang in Paris .
