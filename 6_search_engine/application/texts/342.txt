The WSR-1 or Weather Surveillance Radar-1 was one of the first weather radars . The WSR-1 series was a modified version of the AN/APS-2F radar , which the Weather Bureau ( now the National Weather Service ) acquired from the Navy . The WSR-1A , WSR-3 , and WSR-4 were also variants of this radar . The first WSR-1 in the USA was at Washington National Airport in Washington , D.C. # Radar Sites #
