Frances Ann Stewart ( ne Carkeek ) ( June 18 , 1840 - November 12 , 1916 ) was a social activist in New Zealand . She was the first female member of a New Zealand hospital board . Stewart was born in Sydney , Australia in 1839 or 1840 . Her parents were Stephen and Martha Carkeek . Her father commanded the revenue cutter ' ' Ranger ' ' . In 1865 , she married the surveyor , John Tiffin Stewart .
