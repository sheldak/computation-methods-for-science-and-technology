The unicorn grenadier ' ' Coelorinchus productus ' ' is a species of rattail . This fish is found at depths of up to 600 m in the waters around northern Taiwan , southern Japan and the East China Sea . This fish grows to a length of about 31 cm and is generally brown above , silvery below , with blackish fins and mouth and gill cavities . It can be distinguished from its congeners by its fairly long , pointed snout , teeth restricted to the central premaxilla and by only having a small ventral bioluminescent organ .
