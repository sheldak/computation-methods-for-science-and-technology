Dysembryoplastic neuroepithelial tumour , commonly abbreviated DNT or DNET , is a type of brain tumour . Defined as an usually supratentorial glial-neuronal neoplasm occurring in children and young adults and characterized by a predominantly cortical location and by drug-resistant partial seizures It appears similar to oligodendroglioma , but with visible neurons . It is a benign tumor ( Grade I of the WHO classification of brain tumours ) . It can cause epilepsy. # Treatment # Treatment of DNTs is usually surgical resection .
