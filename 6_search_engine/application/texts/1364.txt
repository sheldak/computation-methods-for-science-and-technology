birthplace = Saint-Cloud , France currentclub = Clermont Rmy Dugimont ( born 1 July 1986 in Saint-Cloud ) is a French football player who currently plays for French club Clermont Foot in the Ligue 2 . He plays as a striker and began his career with professional club Paris Saint-Germain . After three years at the club , Dugimont signed with amateur club Le Chesnay and later joined Levallois SC . He spent one season at the club before joining Poissy in the Championnat de France amateur 2 helping the club earn promotion to the fourth division . During the 201011 season , Dugimont 's amateur career has been covered extensively through a blog by French newspaper and website L'Equipe and its sister site France Football . # References #
