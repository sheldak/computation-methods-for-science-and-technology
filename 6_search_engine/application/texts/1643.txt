The Cambodian Laughingthrush ( ' ' Garrulax ferrarius ' ' ) is a species of bird in the Leiothrichidae family . It formerly was considered conspecific with the White-necked Laughingthrush , ' ' G. strepitans ' ' . It is found in southwestern Cambodia . Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and subtropical or tropical moist montane forests .
