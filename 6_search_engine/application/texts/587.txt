Bernard Bernie Hartigan ( born 1943 ) is an Irish retired hurler who played as a midfielder for the Limerick senior team . Hartigan made his first appearance for the team during the 1964 championship and was a regular member of the starting for the next decade . During that time he won one All-Ireland medal , two Munster medals and one National Hurling League medal . At club level Hartigan was a one-time county football championship medalist with Old Christians . Hartigans brother , Pat , was also an All-Ireland medaist with Limerick .
