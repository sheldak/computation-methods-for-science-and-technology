birthplace= Sioux City , IA Edward Donald Stone ( born January 5 , 1937 in Sioux City , Iowa ) was an American college and professional football player . A halfback , Stone played college football at the University of Arkansas , and played professionally in the American Football League for the Denver Broncos from 1961 through 1964 , and then for the AFL 's Buffalo Bills and Houston Oilers . He was an American Football League All-Star in 1961.
