Gilles Lelievre is a French slalom canoer who competed from the late 1980s to the mid-1990s . He won three gold medals in the C-2 team event at the ICF Canoe Slalom World Championships , earning them in 1987 , 1989 and 1991 . He also won the overall World Cup title in C-2 in 1989 . His partner in the boat throughout the whole of his active career was Jrme Daille.
