category = Warszawa Wola Grzybowska railway station is a railway station in the Wesoa district of Warsaw , Poland . As of 2012 , it is served by Koleje Mazowieckie , who run the KM2 services from Warszawa Zachodnia to ukw and by Szybka Kolej Miejska , who run the S2 services from Warszawa Zachodnia to Sulejwek Miosna.
