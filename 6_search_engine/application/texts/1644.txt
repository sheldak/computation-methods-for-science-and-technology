' ' Lepidochrysops cinerea ' ' is a butterfly in the Lycaenidae family . It is found in the Democratic Republic of Congo , Tanzania and Zambia . The habitat consists of ' ' Brachystegia ' ' woodland and montane grassland at altitudes between 1,000 and 2,000 meters . Adults are on wing from August to September in montane grassland and from November to December in woodland .
