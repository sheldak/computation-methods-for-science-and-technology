Eugen Peder Lunde ( May 18 , 1887 June 17 , 1963 ) was a Norwegian sailor who competed in the 1924 Summer Olympics . In 1924 he won the gold medal as a crew member of the Norwegian boat ' ' Elisabeth V ' ' in the 6 metre class event . # Personal # Father of Peder Lunde , Father in law of Vibeke Lunde , Grandfather of Peder Lunde , Jr . and Great-grand father of Jeanette Lunde all Olympic sailors for Norway .
