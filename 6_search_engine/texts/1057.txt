40269514 Apam balik ( may be known as ' Turnover Pancake ' in English ) or Ban Jian Kuih , Chin Loong Pau , Martabak Manis , Terang Bulan , Apam Pulau Pinang or Kuih Haji ( in certain areas ) is a type of griddle cake . Apam Balik is usually sold at specialist roadside stalls throughout Indonesia and Malaysia . The texture of Apam Balik is akin to a crispier form of crumpets . The Peranakan version called Apom Balik closely resembles the Indonesian ' ' Serabi ' ' .
