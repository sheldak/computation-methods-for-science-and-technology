18898514 Monterey National Forest was established as the Monterey Forest Reserve by the U.S. Forest Service in California on June 25 , 1906 with 335195 acre . It became a National Forest on March 4 , 1907 . On July 1 , 1908 Pinnacles National Forest and San Benito National Forest were added . On August 8 , 1919 the entire forest was transferred to Santa Barbara National Forest and the name was discontinued . The lands presently exist in Los Padres National Forest .
