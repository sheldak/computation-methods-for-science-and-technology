881514 Davoud Soleymani ( ) is an Iranian politician . Soleymani was the Vice Minister of Studental Affairs in Ministry of Science , Research and Technology under the ministrant of former Minister Mostafa Moin . He was also a representative from Tehran on sixth term of Iranian Islamic Parliament , Majlis . He is also member of former front runner political party of Iran , Islamic Iran Participation Front . He is Also member of Islamic Iran Participation Front 's Central Council . He was , in 2001 , a member of Parliament 's Cultural Commission .
