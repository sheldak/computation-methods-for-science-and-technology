31357514 ' ' Ayu-mi-x 7 : Version Acoustic Orchestra ' ' is a remix album released by Japanese pop singer Ayumi Hamasaki on April 20 , 2011 , alongside the other remix albums of Ayu-mi-x 7 project . This is her seventh entry in the ' ' Ayu-mi-x ' ' remix album series , and her first ' ' Ayu-mi-x ' ' album since 2008 's Ayu-mi-x 6 ( it was released in Gold and Silver version ) . Album was originally scheduled to be released on March 30 , but it was postponed due to the 2011 Tohoku earthquake and tsunami and was released on April 20 .
