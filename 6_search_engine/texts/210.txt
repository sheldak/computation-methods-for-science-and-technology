5514514 The Type 89 torpedo ( formerly GRX-2 ) is a Japanese submarine-launched homing torpedo produced by Mitsubishi heavy industries . It was developed in the early 1980s , and is similar in capabilities to the U.S. Mark 48 ADCAP torpedo . It entered service in the 1990s and is currently carried aboard the Harushio , Oyashio and ' ' Sry ' ' -class submarine . It was also carried by the Yuushio-class submarines before they were retired from active service in 2006 .
