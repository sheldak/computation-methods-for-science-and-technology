37929514 Hugh Lafferty ( 1901-1970 ) was a Scottish association football half back who played professionally in Scotland , England and the United States . Lafferty spent time with teams in both Scotland ( St Johnstone ) and England ( Fulham ) , who later progressed to their countries respective Premier Leagues , before moving to the United States in 1929 to join the New York Nationals of the American Soccer League . In 1930 , the Nationals were renamed the Giants and Lafferty played for them through the 1931 season . He was married with three children and retired to Kilsyth in Scotland . He came from a footballing family , his great-uncle Pat Lafferty having played for Glasgow Rangers in 1886 , becoming the first Catholic player to do so .
