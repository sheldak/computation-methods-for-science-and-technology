33963514 The 1977 British motorcycle Grand Prix was the thirteenth and final round of the 1977 Grand Prix motorcycle racing season . It took place on 13-14 August 1977 at Silverstone Circuit . 1977 marked the beginning of a new era as , it was the first time the event was held on the British mainland after the Isle of Man TT had represented the United Kingdom on the FIM Grand Prix calendar for the previous 28 years since the championship 's inception in 1949 .
