11309514 Theodore Albert Peyser ( February 18 , 1873 August 8 , 1937 ) was a Democratic member of the United States House of Representatives from New York . # Biography # Peyser was born in Charleston , West Virginia on February 18 , 1873 . He moved to Cincinnati , Ohio in 1893 and worked as a traveling salesman until 1900 when he moved to New York City and entered the life insurance business .
