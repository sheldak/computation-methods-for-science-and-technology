24909514 Seyed Kamal Sajjadi is an Iranian diplomat and politician . Sajjadi is the spokesman for the Followers of Imam Khomeini and ( Supreme ) Leader ( Ayatollah Seyyed Ali Khamenei ) Front . He is Secretary-General of the Islamic Society of Employees , head of the Iran-Vietnam Friendship Association and an employee of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs . He served as Iran 's Ambassador to the Philippines from 1987 to 1991 and Iran 's Ambassador to Vietnam from 1995 to 1999 . Sajjadi has also been Iran 's Accredited Ambassador to Cambodia and director of Iran 's representative office in Brunei .
