22149514 Ogre Municipality ( ) is a municipality in Vidzeme , Latvia . The municipality was formed in 2002 by merging Ogre town and Ogresgals parish . In 2009 it absorbed Krape parish , eipene parish , Laubere parish , Madliena parish , Mazozoli parish , Meele parish , Suntai parish and Taurupe parish the administrative centre being Ogre. # See also # * Administrative divisions of Latvia ( 2009 ) # References #
