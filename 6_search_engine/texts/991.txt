37675514 coordinates = From 2000 - 2012 , the Emerald Theatre ( now the Macomb Music Theatre ) was a live mixed-use entertainment and concert venue located in downtown Mt . Clemens , Michigan . In July 2012 , the Emerald Theatre was padlocked in foreclosure by Talmer Bank and Trust , closing the venue . On November 5 , 2012 , the theater was purchased by Wally Mona , a metropolitan Detroit restaurant and nightclub owner , and Marc Beginin , a Michigan-based entertainment attorney , who have begun renovations of the historic property at an anticipated cost in excess of $2 million . Under new ownership , it was renamed the Macomb Music Theatre . Renovations are currently underway and the venue is anticipated to be reopen in 2013.
