11854514 The Party for European Future ( Macedonian : , ' ' Partija za Evropska Idnina ' ' ) is a political party in the Republic of Macedonia which largely represents the Macedonian Muslims . At the last legislative elections , 5 July 2006 , the party won 1 out of 120 seats . The party ran on the elections held on 1 June 2008 , and again won 1 seat in the parliament . In the 2011 elections , it lost that seat . Leader of the party is Fijat Canoski.
