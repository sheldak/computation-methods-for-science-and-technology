36843514 Scarborough West was a provincial electoral district in Ontario , Canada . It was created prior to the 1963 provincial election and eliminated in 1996 , when its territory was incorporated into the riding of Scarborough Southwest . Scarborough West riding was created from part of the former riding of YorkScarborough . It was in the former borough of Scarborough . Four Members of Provincial Parliament represented the riding during its history .
