17614514 The Deal School District is a community public school district that serves students in Kindergarten through eighth grade from Deal , in Monmouth County , New Jersey , United States . As of the 2008-09 school year , the district 's one school had an enrollment of 96 students and 12.3 classroom teachers ( on an FTE basis ) , for a studentteacher ratio of 7.8 . For grades 9-12 , students attend Asbury Park High School in neighboring Asbury Park as part of a sending/receiving relationship with the Asbury Park Public Schools . The school was established in September 1953 . As of the 2002-03 school year , nearly 75% of students at Deal School attended from outside the district on a tuition basis , paying $4,300 per student , per year .
