13776514 The Alturas Indian Rancheria is a federally recognized tribe of Achomawi Indians in California . The tribe controls 20acre m2 reservation near Alturas , California , in Modoc County . Tribal enrollment is estimated at 15 . The tribe operates the Desert Rose Casino and the Rose Cafe in Alturas . The reservation lies about one mile southeast of downtown Alturas . The Achomawi are also known as the Pit River Indians . They traditionally spoke the Achumawi language .
