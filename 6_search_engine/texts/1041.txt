39687514 footnotes = The Divan Istanbul ( ) is a five star hotel in Istanbul , Turkey . It is located next to Taksim Gezi Park in Elmada , ili , in walking distance from Taksim Square . Opened in 1956 , it is the flagship of the Divan Group hotel chain . During the 2013 protests in Turkey it opened its doors to injured protestors as well as those fleeing police attacks . During the night of 15/16 June , police repeatedly tear-gassed the lobby . As a result , according to Turkish Prime Minister Recep Tayyip Erdoan , the hotel had helped criminals . # References #
