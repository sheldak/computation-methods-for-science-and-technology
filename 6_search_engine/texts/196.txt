5257514 archipelago = Denham Island is part of the Great Barrier Reef Marine Park at the tip of Cape Melville , Queensland in Bathurst Bay . It is the southernmost island in the Flinders Group National Park . It is south of Flinders Island . It is around 390 hectares or 3.9 square km in size . Aboriginal mythology ( Cooktown ) states Denham Island as a major Moon-Myth site , believed to have been originally joined to Bathurst Heads but later pushed out by the moon .
