5701514 Kaur Kender , ( born May 27 , 1971 in Estonia ) is an Estonian author and entrepreneur . An advertising executive by profession , Kender entered the Estonian literary scene in 1998 with his debut novel Independence Day ( Iseseisvuspev ) . Since then his books have enjoyed unprecedented popularity in Estonia with the old and the young , the literary elite and the popular culture .
