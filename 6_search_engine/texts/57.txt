1324514 Michele Ferrero ( ) ( born 26 April 1925 ) is the owner of the eponymous chocolate maker Ferrero SpA , one of Europe 's largest with estimated 2012 sales of $19 billion . Ferrero , who was born in Dogliani , Italy , is Italy 's richest person , with a personal wealth of $26 billion , Brands include Nutella , Mon Chri , Kinder Chocolate , Ferrero Rocher , Tic Tacs and Kinder Eggs . Since 1997 , his sons , Giovanni Ferrero and Pietro Ferrero , have led the company . Pietro died on 18 April 2011 in an accident in South Africa .
