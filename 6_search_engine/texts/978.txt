37174514 Linda Nordlund ( born 26 November 1986 ) is a Swedish politician and the current chairperson of the Liberal Youth of Sweden . She succeeded Adam Cwejman as chairperson after the 2012 Liberal Youth of Sweden congress in Uppsala . Before she was elected chairperson she was the vice chairperson of the organisation for several years . Linda holds a Bachelor of Political Science from Uppsala University , and was an intern at the Swedish representation in the UN . # References #
