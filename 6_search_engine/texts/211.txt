5554514 That Girl is a 1981 soul single by American Motown singer-songwriter Stevie Wonder . The song was the leading single from Wonder 's album-era greatest-hits compilation , ' ' Stevie Wonder 's Original Musiquarium I ' ' , as one of four newer songs from the collection . The song spent nine weeks at number one on the ' ' Billboard ' ' R&amp;B singles chart and reached number four on the Hot 100 . The song would later be sampled by rapper 2Pac on his song , So Many Tears and covered by R&amp;B singer Joe . The song was sampled by Queen Latifah on her album ' ' Order in the Court ' ' on the non-U.S . track Let Her Live . A cover version appears on UK band Hue and Cry 's 1992 album Truth &amp; Love .
