39597514 Kimberley College ( also known as STEM College ) is a new free school sixth form centre that opened in Stewartby , Bedfordshire , England in September 2013 . The college is operated by Wootton Academy Trust , who also operate Wootton Upper School . Kimberley College specialises in STEM ( Science , Technology , Engineering and Mathematics ) , and offers a range of A levels and BTECs related to the specialisms . The college accepts students from Bedford , Bletchley and Milton Keynes , as well as the villages in between these areas .
