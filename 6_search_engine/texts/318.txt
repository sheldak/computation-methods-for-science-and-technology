Seychelles , officially the Republic of Seychelles , is an archipelago nation of 115 islands in the Indian Ocean , some 1500 km east of mainland Africa , northeast of the island of Madagascar . Other nearby island countries and territories include Zanzibar to the west , Mauritius and Runion to the south , Comoros and Mayotte to the southwest . The Seychelles has the smallest population of any African state .
