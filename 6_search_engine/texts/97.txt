2366514 Faridhoo ( Dhivehi : ) is one of the inhabited islands of Haa Dhaalu Atoll administrative division and geographically part of Thiladhummathi Atoll in the north of the Maldives . Faridhoo has the highest natural point in the Maldives at 3m above sea level . This makes the Maldives the country with the lowest highest point . It is believed that Buddhist ruins from the pre-Islamic era are buried at this site .
