During the summer and autumn of 1989 , Pornapti was a popular transit point for refugees fleeing the socialist eastern European countries , especially East Germany . Its location , far from official border crossing stations and surrounded on three sides by Austria made it a practical pedestrian escape route . Many automobiles were abandoned in the woods to the northeast .
