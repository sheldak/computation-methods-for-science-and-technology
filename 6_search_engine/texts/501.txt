16592514 Established in 1966 , the Population Research Laboratory is a centre for social science research at the University of Alberta in Edmonton . With close to 120 employees , the PRL is the largest academic centre of its kind in Western Canada . It has extensive experience in conducting research in the areas of health , education , labour markets , environment , science and technology , immigration , social policy and public opinion .
