29334514 Cadwalader Beach ( ) is a beach nearly long at the south end of Beaufort Island , in the Ross Archipelago . The beach is occupied by a large Adlie penguin rookery and there is easy access from the sea when the coast is ice free . It was named by the New Zealand Geological Survey Antarctic Expedition ( 195859 ) for Captain John Cadwalader , U.S. Navy , who encouraged and assisted the expedition in its Antarctic program , and also rendered valuable assistance to the New Zealand parties of the Commonwealth Trans-Antarctic Expedition , 195658.
