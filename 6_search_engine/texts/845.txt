31108514 Charlie Lyne ( born 1991 ) is the editor of Ultra Culture , a UK-based movie blog , and a contributor to the BBC 's ' ' The Film Programme ' ' . # Ultra Culture # Established in 2008 when Lyne was 16 , Ultra Culture is a film blog known for its chatty style , strong opinions and concise pop cultural commentary . According to critic aggregation website Rotten Tomatoes , the blog agrees with popular opinion 66% of the time . # ' ' The Film Programme ' ' # Lyne joined the programme when it was redeveloped after Jonathan Ross 's departure . New host Claudia Winkleman approached him using Twitter , saying that she was ' obsessed ' with his website and that she would ' bloody kill ' for him to be on the show .
