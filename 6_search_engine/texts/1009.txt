38639514 subdivisiontype1 = Province Ougarta is an oasis and a village in the commune of Bni Abbs , in Bchar Province , Algeria . It is 50 km southwest of the town of Bni Abbs and about 250 km south of Bchar , the capital of the province . Ougarta has a population of about 250 inhabitants . The village has given its name to the Ougarta Range which surrounds the village .
