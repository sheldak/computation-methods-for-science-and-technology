8305514 birthplace = Buenos Aires , Argentina Hernn Losada ( born May 9 , 1982 , in Buenos Aires ) is an Argentine professional football midfielder . He currently plays for Lierse S.K. in Belgium . Losada started his career at Club Atltico Independiente in the Primera Division Argentina in 2003 . In 2005 he was sold to Universidad de Chile but he only made six appearances for the club before signing a four-year deal with Germinal in 2006 . He then went on to sign a four-season contract with the Belgian club RSC Anderlecht in May 2008 . Losada made a major impact in only his fourth game in Belgian football , when he scored a hat-trick against Excelsior Mouscron in a comprehensive 62 victory .
