31530514 ' ' Umayodus ' ' is an extinct genus of condylarth mammal from the late Paleocene or the earliest Eocene . It is a didolodontid which lived in what is now Peru . It is known from the holotype LU3-801 , an isolated right third molar , which was found in the Muami Formation of Laguna Umayo , Peru . It was first named by Javier N. Gelfo and Bernard Sig in 2011 and the type species is ' ' Umayodus raimondi ' ' . # Phylogeny # Cladogram after Gelfo and Sig , 2011 : label3=Didolodontidae
