12598514 The spotback skate ( ' ' Atlantoraja castelnaui ' ' ) is a species of fish in the Rajidae family . It is found in Argentina , Brazil , and Uruguay . Its natural habitat is open seas . # Sustainable consumption # In 2010 , Greenpeace International has added the spotback skate to its seafood red list . The Greenpeace International seafood red list is a list of fish that are commonly sold in supermarkets around the world , and which have a very high risk of being sourced from unsustainable fisheries .
