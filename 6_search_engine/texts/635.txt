22138514 birthplace = Eskifjrdur , Iceland Valur Fannar Gslason ( born 8 September 1977 in Eskifjrdur ) is an Icelandic international footballer who last played for Haukar . He is the elder brother of fellow international player Stefn Gslason. # Career # Valur moved from Fram to English side Arsenal in 1996 . However , he never made a league appearance for Arsenal , and spent a loan spell at Brighton &amp; Hove Albion in 1997 . He moved to Norwegian side Strmsgodset IF in 1998 , before returning to Fram in 1999 . He later played for the Iceland national football team and captained for Fram . He later played mostly for Fylkir.
