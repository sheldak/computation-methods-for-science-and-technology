35284514 ' ' Black Sky : The Race For Space ' ' is a Discovery Channel documentary about Space Ship One , and how a small team backed by Paul Allen achieved human suborbital spaceflight and win the Ansari X Prize . It contain insights about how the rocketplane was built , the challenge they faced when they flew it , the vision of Burt Rutan about the future of this technology ( tier two and three ) , and his thoughts about NASA and government .
