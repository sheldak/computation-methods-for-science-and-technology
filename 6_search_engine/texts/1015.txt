38808514 architect = governingbody = Private The Thomas and Mary Hepworth House , at 725 West 200 North in Salt Lake City , Utah , is a two-story Italianate style I-house plan house that was built in 1877 . It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2000 . It is the only known example of 2-story , Italianate I-house design in Salt Lake City . It is significant architecturally as an expression of traditional central-passage plan houses in Utah , but also updated with more vertical Victorian architecture
