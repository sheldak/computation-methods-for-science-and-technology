35125514 Donald M. Finlayson ( August 9 , 1854 ) was a farmer and political figure in Saskatchewan . He represented North Battleford from 1908 to 1917 and Jack Fish Lake from 1917 to 1934 in the Legislative Assembly of Saskatchewan as a Liberal . He was born in Elgin County , Ontario , the son of Duncan Finlayson and Annabel Matheson , both natives of Scotland . He moved to Battleford , Saskatchewan in 1879 . In 1881 , Finlayson married his wife Katie . Their home was ravaged during the North-West Rebellion of 1885 but the family were not at home , having been warned in advance . Finlayson served with local militia during the rebellion . He was president of the North Battleford Agricultural Society . # References #
