36645514 ' ' Sturm und Drang ' ' is a play in five acts by Friedrich Maximilian Klinger , which gave its name to the artistic period known as Sturm und Drang . It premiered in Leipzig on 1 April 1777 when it was performed by Abel Seyler 's famed theatrical company , where Klinger was employed as a playwright for two years . # Literature # * Karthaus , Ulrich : Sturm und Drang .
