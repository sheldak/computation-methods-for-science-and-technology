35668514 in Verda Louisiana Highway 471 ( LA 471 ) is a state highway in Louisiana that serves Grant and Winn Parishes . It spans 17.81 mi in a south-to-north direction . The highway is also paired with LA 122 for a short distance . # Route description # From the south , LA 471 begins at an intersection with US 71 north of Colfax . It travels through timber lands and hills before meeting LA 122 south of Verda . LA 122 and LA 471 are paired together for the next mile , where LA 122 splits to the west , while LA 471 turns north . LA 471 then travels into Winn Parish before ending at an intersection with LA 34 south of Atlanta . LA 471 is an undivided , two-lane highway for its entire length . Before the 1955 renumbering , LA 471 was State Route 5 . The importance of State Route 5 was that it carried the original US 167 to its terminus at US 71 , which is also LA 471 's southern terminus .
