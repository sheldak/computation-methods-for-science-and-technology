19518514 The 2nd Health Support Battalion ( 2HSB ) is a unit of the 17th Combat Service Support Brigade . It is based at the Gallipoli Barracks in Enoggera , Queensland . The battalion is rapidly deployable to provide field medical support for land-based forces . This includes initial wound surgery , resuscitation and medium to high intensity nursing care , ( surgical resuscitation ) and medium to high intensity nursing care in the area of operations .
