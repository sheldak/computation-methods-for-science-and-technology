1553514 Sannieshof is a small farming town situated in North West Province of South Africa . It started as a post office to serve the farms in the district and was named in honour of the first postmaster , John Voorendijk 's wife Sannie . It is also close ( 20 km ) to Baberspan which is a water bird sanctuary and a fishing mecca . Village 40 km north-east of Delareyville and 38 km north-west of Ottosdal . The name was bestowed by John Voorendijk who was postmaster of Lichtenburg , in honour of his wife Sannie ( ne De Beer ) , whom he married in 1904.
