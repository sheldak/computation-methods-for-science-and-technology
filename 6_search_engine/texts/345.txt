10609514 subdivisiontype1 = Governorate Al-Suqaylabiyah ( ' ' ' ' ) is a Greek Orthodox Christian Syrian city administratively belonging to Hama Governorate . Al-Suqaylabiyah is located at a height of 220 meters above sea level . According to the 2004 official census , the town has a population of 17,313. # History # The name goes back to the ancient Seleucia ad Belum , a town of Hellenistic foundation that was located almost at the same place . The site was given up during the middle ages and repopulated at the beginning of the 19th century . In 1860 local Bedouin tribes attacked al-Suqaylabiyah.
