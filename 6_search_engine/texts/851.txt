31161514 Koda Kumi Remix Album is the first remix album by Japanese singer Kumi Koda . It was released on February 22 , 2006 , on the same day as her last single from 12-singles project - Someday/Boys &amp; Girls . It was available only to those who bought all 12 singles from Best : Second Session in digital-only format . ' ' Koda Kumi Remix Album ' ' was Kumi 's first remix album before ' ' Koda Kumi Driving Hit 's ' ' .
