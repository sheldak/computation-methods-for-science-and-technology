42181514 The 2014 Eastern Michigan Eagles football team will represent Eastern Michigan University in the 2014 NCAA Division I FBS football season . The Eagles play their home games at Rynearson Stadium and are members of the West Division of the Mid-American Conference . This season will see Eastern Michigan play on a new gray FieldTurf surface at Rynearson Stadium .
