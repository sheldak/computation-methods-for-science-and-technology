40718514 birthplace = Martin , Czechoslovakia Michaela Matejov ( born 2 March 1986 in Martin , Czechoslovakia ) is a Slovakian ice hockey defender . # International career # Matejov was selected for the Slovakia national women 's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics . She played in all four games , but did not record a point . She played in the qualifying campaigns for the 2010 and 2014 Olympics . Matejov has also appeared for Slovakia at six IIHF Women 's World Championships across three levels . Her first appearance came in 2005 .
