17879514 The World Chart Express chart was a show played on MTV Europe every Saturday , repeating then on Sundays and Fridays . It was a top ten chart , consisting of popular songs across the world . It was one of the few charts that actually plays music sung in different languages other than English , representing what 's truly popular in each country . It had an Indian version which was played on a satellite TV network called Tata Sky for 5 weeks .
