2935514 South Creake is a village and civil parish in north west Norfolk , England . The village lies south of its twin village of North Creake , and some five miles ( 8 km ) north-west of the town of Fakenham . South Creake lies on the River Burn , which flows through the centre of the village . On a hill above the village can be found the Bloodgate Hill Iron Age Fort . The civil parish has an area of and in the 2001 census had a population of 536 in 245 households . For the purposes of local government , the parish falls within the district of King 's Lynn and West Norfolk . # Notes #
