30121514 ' ' Lost Ships ' ' is an accessory for the ' ' Advanced Dungeons &amp; Dragons ' ' fantasy role-playing game . # Contents # ' ' Lost Ships ' ' is a Spelljammer supplement which describes a sort of Sargasso Sea in space . In this place , derelict ships gather and weird creatures abound , and some things have lain asleep there for a long time . This supplement also features new monsters , spells , and ships , plus miniscenarios. # Publication history # SJR1 ' ' Lost Ships ' ' was written by Ed Greenwood , with a cover by Brom , and was published by TSR in 1990 as a 96-page book . # Reception # # Reviews #
