36916514 Bronwyn Marshall ( born 31 December 1963 ) is a former Australian women 's basketball player . # Biography # Marshall played for the national team between 1982 and 1987 , competing at the 1984 Olympic Games in Los Angeles . Marshall also represented Australia at two World Championships ; 1983 held in Brazil and 1986 held in the Soviet Union . In the domestic Women 's National Basketball League ( WNBL ) Marshall played 121 games for the Brisbane Blazers . In her post-competitive career , Marshall has worked as the Executive Officer of the Queensland Branch of Sports Medicine Australia , Table Tennis Queensland , and Table Tennis Australia , and as the High Performance Coordinator for Softball Queensland . Marshall is also currently a member of the Basketball Australia High Performance Commission and a member of the Basketball Queensland board .
