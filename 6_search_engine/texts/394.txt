12522514 Harar saga ok Hlmverja or Harar saga og Hlmverja ( ) is one of the sagas of Icelanders . It is preserved in two medieval , vellum manuscripts , Reykjavk , Stofnun rna Magnssonar , AM 556a , ff . 70r-88r ( from c. 1475 ) , and the fragment AM 564A , 4:o ( ' Vatnshyrna ' , c. 1400 ) , f. 7 . It also survives in a further thirty-seven paper manuscripts , all descended from AM 556a 4to. # Notes #
