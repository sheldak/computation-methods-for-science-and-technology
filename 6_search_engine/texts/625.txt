21873514 ' ' Chenopodium leptophyllum ' ' is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family known by the common name narrowleaf goosefoot . It is native to much of western North America , where it is reported from Alaska to Texas and northern Mexico , and into central Canada . It can be found in many types of open habitat , often in sandy and gravelly soils , and it grows easily in disturbed areas such as roadsides . It is an erect or mostly erect annual herb approaching 40 to 60 centimeters in maximum height . It is powdery in texture , especially on the undersides of the leaves . The thin , dusty leaf is linear to narrowly lance-shaped , smooth along the edges , and up to about 2.5 centimeters in length . The inflorescences are located at the tips of branches and in the leaf axils . They are arrays of clusters of tightly packed , tiny flowers .
