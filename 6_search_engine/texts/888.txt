32819514 birthplace = Greenwich , London , England Adam Peter Augustus Clarke ( born 13 September 1984 ) is an English cricketer . Clarke is a right-handed batsman who bowls right-arm medium-fast . He was born in Greenwich , London . While studying for his degree at Durham University , Clarke made his only first-class appearance for Durham UCCE against Northamptonshire in 2004 . In this match , he was dismissed for a duck in the university 's first-innings by Robert White , while with the ball he bowled 5.4 wicket-less overs .
