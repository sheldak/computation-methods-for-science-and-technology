39573514 Grigori Kuzmin ( April 8 , 1917 April 22 , 1988 ) was an Estonian astronomer , who worked mainly in the field of stellar dynamics . # Life and career # Grigori Kuzmin was born in 1917 in Viipuri , part of the Grand Duchy of Finland . His family was Russian . In 1924 they moved to Tallinn , Estonia , where Kuzmin attended school . Although his mother tongue was Russian , and he had learned to speak Finnish during childhood , after moving to Estonia , Estonian became his primary language . Kuzmin graduated cum laude from the University of Tartu in 1940.
