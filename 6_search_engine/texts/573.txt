20013514 Herbert Edward Jones ( 18611920 ) was an English clergyman , the second Suffragan Bishop of Lewes and Archdeacon of Chichester . The second son of Sir Willoughby Jones , 3rd Baronet , he was educated at Eton College and Trinity College , Cambridge , ordained in 1884 and began his career with a curacy at St Andrews , Westminster . He was then successively the incumbent at Knebworth , Petworth and Hitchin before ascending being appointed Archdeacon of Chichester and ordinated as Suffragan Bishop of Lewes in 1914 , posts he held until his death . # Notes #
