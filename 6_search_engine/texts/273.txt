7567514 Natalya Nikolaevna Ivanova ( , born June 25 , 1981 ) is a Russian hurdler . She finished fifth in the 400m hurdles final at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg . She also ran in the heats for Russia 's 4 x 400m relay team in the 2004 Olympics .
