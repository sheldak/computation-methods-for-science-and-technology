3023514 The Centre Mario Gosselin is a multi-purpose arena in Thetford Mines , Quebec . It was built in 1964 and has a capacity of 2,500 . It was first called the Centre des Loisirs but , in the middle of the 80 's , it was renamed for NHL and Team Canada goalie Mario Gosselin , who was born in Thetford Mines and helped Canada finish fourth at the 1984 Winter Olympics . It is home to the Thetford Mines Isothermic of the Ligue Nord-Amricaine de Hockey .
