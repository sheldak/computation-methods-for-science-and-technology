4492514 Julius Jules Levin ( 3 February 1922 &amp;ndash ; 16 May 1988 ) was the Socialist Labor Party of America candidate for United States President in the 1976 Presidential election ; his running mate was Constance Blomen . Levin also ran for Governor of New Jersey five times unsuccessfully ; the party ( headquartered in New Jersey ) had run candidates for governor starting in 1898 . Levin also ran for Senator from New Jersey four times : 1966 , 1972 , 1982 , 1984 and for the New Jersey General Assembly in 1975.
