29654514 Barent van Kalraet , or Barent Pietersz Calraat ( 16 August 1649 - 9 February 1737 ) was a Dutch Golden Age painter and the younger brother of Abraham Calraet. # Biography # Van Kalraet was born and died in Dordrecht . He learned to paint from his brother Abraham , and he later became a pupil of Aelbert Cuyp . He changed his style several times , and in later life took to painting Rhine landscapes in the manner of Herman Saftleven . He was still painting when Houbraken was writing his book , but it was more a hobby than a business for him . He resided throughout his life in Dordrecht , and made copies after the landscape artists Cuyp and Saftleven , but also Jan Both , Cornelis van Poelenburch , Philip Wouwerman and Willem de Heusch.
