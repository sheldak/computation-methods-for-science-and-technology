17640514 Select Sport A.S . is a Danish sports equipment manufacturer based in Glostrup , Denmark . It was founded in 1947 by Eigil Nielsen , the former goalkeeper of the Danish national football team . In the 1950s , Select manufactured and marketed the first 32-panel soccer ball . Based loosely on the geodesic dome designs of R. Buckminster Fuller , As of 2008 , Select provides the Danish Football Association with footballs , and also several clubs in the Danish , Belgian and Russian top football leagues use Select balls . Select balls are used in the Olympic handball games , as well as in the World Championship and in the Danish , Swedish and Spanish men 's handball leagues , among others .
