6812514 Oregon 's 1st congressional district consists of the northwest corner of Oregon . It includes Clatsop , Columbia , Washington , and Yamhill counties , and a portion of southwest Multnomah County in Portland . The district has been represented by Democrat Suzanne Bonamici since 2012 .
