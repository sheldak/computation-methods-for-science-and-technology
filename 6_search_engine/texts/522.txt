The station is 105.9 rail kilometers from the terminus of the line at Kameyama Station . # History # Minose Station opened on April 26 , 1932 on the Japanese Government Railways ( JGR ) Kisei East Line . The line was extended to Owase Station by December 19 , 1934 , and the JGR became the Japan National Railways ( JNR ) after World War 2 . The line was renamed the Kisei Main Line on July 15 , 1959 . The station has been unattended since December 21 , 1983 .
