9732514 Arjun Malhotra is an Indian businessman who co-founded HCL Technologies with Shiv Nadar in 1976 . He is an alumnus of the Indian Institute of Technology , Kharagpur . He was in E&amp;ECE department of 1970 batch of IIT Kharagpur . He also attended the The Doon School in Dehradun and St. Columba 's School in New Delhi . He founded TechSpan in 1998 . TechSpan merged with Headstrong in 2003 . He sold Headstrong to Genpact in 2011 and currently he is doing some advisory consulting as well as serving on the Boards of a number of education based organizations .
