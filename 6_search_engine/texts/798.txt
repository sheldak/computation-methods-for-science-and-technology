28967514 ' ' Shock Troop ' ' ( German title : ' ' Stotrupp 1917 ' ' ) is a 1934 German war film directed by Hans Zberlein and starring Ludwig Schmid-Wildy , Beppo Brem and Max Zankl . It shows German soldiers fighting in the trenches during the First World War . It was based on the novel ' ' Der Glaube an Deutschland ' ' by Hans Zberlein. # Cast # * Ludwig Schmid-Wildy ..
