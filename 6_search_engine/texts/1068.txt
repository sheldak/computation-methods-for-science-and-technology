41028514 The men 's 1500 metre freestyle competition of the swimming events at the 1999 Pan American Games took place on 6 August ( preliminaries ) and 7 August ( finals ) at the Pan Am Pool . The last Pan American Games champion was Carlton Bruner of US . This race consisted of thirty lengths of the pool , all lengths being in freestyle. # Results # All times are in minutes and seconds . # Heats # The first round was held on August 6. # Final # The final was held on August 7.
