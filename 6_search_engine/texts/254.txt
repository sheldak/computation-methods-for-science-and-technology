7078514 Anvil Press Poetry is an independent poetry publisher based in Greenwich , south-east London . It was founded in 1968 by Peter Jay and specialises in contemporary English poets , with a leavening of Irish and American , and in a range of translated poetry , from ancient classics to modern and contemporary poets . The company 's staff is at its full complement of two people : Peter Jay ( founder , editorial and production director ) and Kit Yee Wong ( administration , rights )
