Winmar is the second cousin of former St Kilda and Western Bulldogs player Nicky Winmar . St Kilda drafted him with the 32nd selection in the 2009 AFL Draft from Claremont Football Club in the West Australian Football League ( WAFL ) . Winmar made his debut in Round 9 of the 2011 AFL season against the Melbourne Football Club and played the following week against Fremantle Football Club before being dropped back to the Victorian Football League ( VFL ) . Winmar was then named as an emergency several times towards the end of the 2011 season . On Tuesday , the 27th of November , the Saints announced they had delisted Winmar . St Kilda 's head of football , Chris Pelchen , said Winmar was contracted until the end of 2013 , however , the club advised the 21-year-old that he would be released .
