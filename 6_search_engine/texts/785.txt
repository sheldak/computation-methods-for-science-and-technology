28178514 The Austrian Open Kitzbhel ( formerly known as the Generali Open ) is an annual tennis tournament held in Kitzbhel , Austria . The event was part of the ATP World Series from the creation of ATP World Tour till 1998 , International Series Gold from 19992008 and ATP World Tour 250 series in 2009 . It was a part of the ATP Challenger Tour in 2010 before regaining top tour status . It is once again part of the 250 series .
