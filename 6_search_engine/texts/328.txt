9631514 birthplace = Pernik , Bulgaria Kiril Lozanov Ivkov ( ; born 21 June 1946 ) is a former Bulgarian football player in defender role . In his career he played for Levski Sofia . In 1974 and 1975 he was named Bulgarian Footballer of the Year . For the Bulgaria national football team he played 44 matches , scoring one goal , and appeared in editions of FIFA World Cup in 1974 . He also played for Bulgaria at the 1968 Summer Olympics . # Awards # * Holder of the Bulgarian Cup - 5 times - 1970,1971,1976,1977,1979 * Champion of Bulgaria - 5 times - 1968,1970,1974,1977,1979
