30999514 Defensive meritorious badge ( Japanese : ) is the decorative insignia for the Members of Japan Self-Defense Forces . The Officials who have been won Special Encomium , 1st Encomium , 2nd Encomium , or 3rd Encomium are given as the supplementary prize . # Sorts # The shape is the rays of the rising sun which is vertically long . If the several badges were given , the winner must wear the only highest badge .
