5761514 Matti Kurki , in Swedish ' ' Matts Kurck ' ' , was a legendary Finnish chieftain . If he were a real person , he is thought to have lived in the late 13th century . Folklore tales tell about Matti Kurki , particularly in the Satakunta province ( Vesilahti , Pirkkala ) but also in Lapland . The first written reference to Matti Kurki dates from the 17th century . The best known tale concerns his duel with a ' ' Pohto ' ' , a Russian-Karelian warrior . The battle would have taken place near the Manor of Laukko . Matti Kurki was supposedly the ancestor of the Finnish noble family of Kurki .
