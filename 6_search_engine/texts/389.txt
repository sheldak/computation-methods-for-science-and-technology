12370514 Constance of Toulouse was the daughter of Raymond VI , Count of Toulouse and his second wife Beatrice of Bziers . She first married Sancho VII of Navarre in 1195 , but they were divorced in 1200 . After the annulment she remarried to Peter Bermond II of Sauve .
