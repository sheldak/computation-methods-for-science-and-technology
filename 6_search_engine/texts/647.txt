22669514 The article presents the discography of American folk music singer-songwriter Tracy Chapman . She has released 8 studio albums , 1 greatest hits album , and 22 singles on Elektra Records . She has 4 Platinum albums , 2 of which are multi-platinum , and 2 Gold albums . Her smash hits Fast Car and Give Me One Reason both hit the Top 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 charts , peaking at #6 and #3 , respectively .
