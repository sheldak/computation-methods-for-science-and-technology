22319514 ' ' The View from Here : Conversations with Gay and Lesbian Filmmakers ' ' is a book by Canadian film journalist Matthew Hays , published in 2007 by Arsenal Pulp Press . Filmmakers interviewed in the book include John Waters , Pedro Almodvar , Gus Van Sant , John Cameron Mitchell , Don Roos , Randal Kleiser , Don Mancini , Kenneth Anger , Gregg Araki , La Pool , Wakefield Poole , Monika Treut , Rosa von Praunheim , John Greyson , Bruce LaBruce , Robert Lepage , Patricia Rozema , David Secter , Lynne Fernie and Aerlyn Weissman . The book won a Lambda Literary Award in 2008 in the category of LGBT Arts and Culture .
