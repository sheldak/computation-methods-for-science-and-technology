21749514 Jim Hrtull ( born March 11 , 1990 ) is a Finnish Nordic combined skier who has been competing since 2007 . At the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2009 in Liberec , he finished eighth in the 4 x 5 km team and 51st in the 10 km individual normal hill events . Hrtull 's best World Cup finish was a 43rd in the 10 km Gundersen event at Klingenthal in February 2009 . His best career finish was 12th in a Gundersen Continental Cup event also held at Klingenthal a month earlier .
