4486514 ( Polish : , Cieszyn Silesian : or ) is a village in Moravian-Silesian Region , Czech Republic , close to the borders with Poland and Slovakia . It has 394 inhabitants ( 2001 census ) , 28.5% of the population are ethnic Poles and 83.7% are Roman Catholics . The village lies in the historical region of Cieszyn Silesia . It is situated on the foothills of the Moravian-Silesian Beskids mountain range . Lomn River flows through the village . # References #
