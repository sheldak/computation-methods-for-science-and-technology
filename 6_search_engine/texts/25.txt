624514 is a district located in Nagano Prefecture , Japan . As of May , 2008 , the district has an estimated Population of 65,359 and a Density of 51.4 persons/km . The total area is 1,270.43 km. # Municipalities # There are 3 towns and 10 villages within the district . * Anan * Matsukawa * Takamori * Achi * Hiraya * Neba * shika * Shimoj * Takagi * Tenry * Toyooka * Urugi * Yasuoka # District Timeline # * 1878 : The district was formed out from Ina District .
