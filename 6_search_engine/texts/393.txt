12489514 The striated yuhina ( ' ' Yuhina castaniceps ' ' ) is a bird species in the White-eye family Zosteropidae . It ranges across the Indian subcontinent and Southeast Asia , including Bangladesh , Bhutan , Hong Kong , India , Laos , Myanmar , Nepal , Thailand , and Vietnam . Its natural habitats are subtropical or tropical moist lowland forests and subtropical or tropical moist montane forests .
