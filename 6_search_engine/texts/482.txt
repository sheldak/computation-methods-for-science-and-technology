15920514 Saint-Sauveur is a commune in the Oise department in northern France . # Mineral water spring # Among the numerous ponds and springs which dot the surrounding Compigne Forest , the Spring of Saint-Sauveur is widely regarded as therapeutic . The spring , which is actually a pair of two separate mineral water springs , runs both hot and cold and is reputed to provide relief for muscular rheumatism and other ailments .
