26543514 ' ' Love in Stero ' ' is a Swedish English language song co-written by Tony Nilsson and Mirja Breitholtz and sang by Swedish singer Ola Svensson during the Melodifestivalen in the 2008 series . The song also appears on ' ' Good Enough - The Feelgood Edition ' ' , the re-release of the singers sophomore studio album . It was first performed on 16 February 2008 in Vsters and then on in Kiruna round as a second chance , but did not make the finals . Although the song did not make to the finals of Melodifestivalen 2008 , it still became very popular in the Swedish Singles Chart .
