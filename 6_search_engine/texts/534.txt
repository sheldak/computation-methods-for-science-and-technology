18020514 birthplace = Villeneuve-Saint-Georges , France position = Striker / Midfielder Samuel Pitre ( born 10 February 1984 in Villeneuve-Saint-Georges ) is a French football player . Currently , he plays in the Championnat National for US Crteil-Lusitanos . He played for the main squad of Paris Saint-Germain F.C . in Coupe de France and UEFA Cup . He was part of the 18-man squad for the junior French national team that won the FIFA U-17 World Cup in 2001.
