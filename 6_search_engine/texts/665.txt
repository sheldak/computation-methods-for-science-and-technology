Oscar Bonsu Amoabeng ( born 31 December 1984 in Accra , Ghana ) is a naturalized Equatoguinean footballer , who plays for K Putte SK. # International career # Amoabeng made his Equatorial Guinea national team debut on 6 September 2008 in a World Cup 2010 Qualifying match against Sierra Leone in Freetown . That day the ' ' Nzalang Nacional ' ' ( the nickname of Equatorial Guinea national football team ) lost by 2-0 . Later , he played other World Cup 2010 Qualifying match ( against South Africa on 11 October 2008 , in Malabo ) .
