24702514 The Long Island Golf Association Amateur Championship , or simply the Long Island Amateur , has been held annually since 1922 in Long Island . The tournament is organized by the Long Island Golf Association and played on a rotating basis at member clubs . Throughout its history , no one has dominated this Championship like Gene Francis . Beginning in 1962 , he won seven times , finished 2nd four times , and was the medalist on four occasions . In more recent years , George Zahringer , John Baldwin , Bob Murphy , Ken Bakst , and Joe Saladino have dominated the Amateur Championship . # Winners #
