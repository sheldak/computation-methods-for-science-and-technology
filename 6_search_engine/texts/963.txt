36639514 Sussex County Cricket Club was established on 1 March 1839 . Since then , they have played first-class and List A cricket at a number of different home grounds . Their first home first-class match was against Kent in 1839 at the Royal New Ground , Brighton . The current headquarters of Sussex County Cricket Club is the County Ground , which located in Hove . This venue has also has held a single One Day International between India and South Africa in the 1999 World Cup . As of 5 August 2012 , Sussex have played 3,345 first-class matches and 919 List A matches at seventeen different home grounds . They have also played 97 Twenty20 matches in England , with 45 of their homes games having been played at the County Ground , Hove , and three at Arundel Castle Cricket Ground .
