6989514 Never Make a Promise is a number-one R&amp;B song by group Dru Hill , released in 1997 . It is the third single from their eponymous debut album . With a lead by Larry Jazz Anthony , the single spent four weeks at number one on the US R&amp;B chart and peaked at number seven on the US pop chart . The music video features an appearance by Michelle Thomas , who plays Jazz 's girlfriend . She tells Jazz that she is pregnant and he gets excited , but she also has a drunk father who rapes her but is too scared to tell anyone . # Charts #
