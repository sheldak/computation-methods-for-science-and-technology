21887514 wiemino ( formerly German ' ' Schwemmin ' ' ) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Biesiekierz , within Koszalin County , West Pomeranian Voivodeship , in north-western Poland . It lies approximately 9km mi south-west of Biesiekierz , 19kmmi 0 south-west of Koszalin , and 117kmmi 0 north-east of the regional capital Szczecin . Before 1945 the area was part of Germany . For the history of the region , see History of Pomerania.
