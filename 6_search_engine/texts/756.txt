26788514 Or is a small island in Denmark located in the Isefjord . It covers 14km2 acre and has a population of 846 ( ) . Administratively the island belongs to Holbk Municipality . There are four villages on Or : Bybjerg , the largest village on the island with a population of 248 ( 1 January 2013 ) , Brnde , Gamlse and Nsby . It is a popular vacation area with some 1,200 summer houses . There is a ferry connection between Or and Holbk , and a cable ferry connects the island to Hammer Bakke ( ' ' Hammer Hill ' ' ) on the Hornsherred peninsula . Or Church dating back to the 12th century and Or Museum depicting local life in the area are national monuments .
