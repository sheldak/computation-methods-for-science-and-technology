21233514 birthplace = Kakanj , Bosnia position = setter Ermin Lepi ( known simply as Lepa ) is a Bosnian volleyball player , currently playing for OK Sisak , a team in the Croatian Premier Volleyball League Men 's Championship ( Superliga ) Division , and also an international player for Bosnia and Herzegovina . Until the end of the 2009-2010 season , Lepi played as setter for OK Kakanj , the Premier League of Volleyball of Bosnia and Herzegovina 's most successful volleyball club 1994-2008 and was team captain 2002-2004 . In September 2010 he decided not to sign for OK Kakanj again on the terms offered . While with OK Kakanj he won 6 Championship titles ( 2000 , 2001 , 2003 , 2004 , 2005 ) and won the National Cup of Bosnia and Herzegovina 9 times ( 1994 , 1995 , 1996 , 1997 , 2001 , 2002 , 2003 , 2004 , 2006 ) .
