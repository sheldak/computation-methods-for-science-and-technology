35673514 The ' ' Hastings Constitutional Law Quarterly ' ' ( ' ' Bluebook ' ' abbreviation : ' ' Hastings Const . L.Q. ' ' ) is a quarterly law review covering constitutional law edited by students of the University of California , Hastings College of the Law . While most articles focus on issues arising under the United States Constitution , the journal also covers topics concerning state and foreign constitutions . It was established in 1973.
