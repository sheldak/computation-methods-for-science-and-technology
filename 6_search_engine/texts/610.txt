21363514 Waowice is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Gubin , within Krosno Odrzaskie County , Lubusz Voivodeship , in western Poland , close to the German border . It lies approximately 7km mi north-east of Gubin , 22kmmi 0 west of Krosno Odrzaskie , and 49kmmi 0 west of Zielona Gra . Before 1945 the area was part of Germany ( see ' ' Territorial changes of Poland after World War II ' ' ) .
