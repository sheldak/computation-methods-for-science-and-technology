12135514 The Arieul Mic River ( ) is a river in the Apuseni Mountains , Alba County , western Romania . It is the right headwater of the river Arie . It flows through the villages Avram Iancu and Vidra , and joins the Arieul Mare ( the other headwater ) near Cmpeni . It is fed by several smaller streams : * Micoiu , Librana , Valea Boului , Drghia , Rul Petelui , Gombo , Ciucileni , Dochi , Slatina and Valea Lung on the left side : * Divaia , Cioha , Pltini , Laz , Chiorceti , Prisaci , Dobrani , Vidrioara and Plecua.
