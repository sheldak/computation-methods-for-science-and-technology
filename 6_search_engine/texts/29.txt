707514 Bankfield Road is the name of a 4.3 km long rural road in the city of Ottawa , Ontario , Canada . It begins at Highway 416 in the west , and runs east to Rideau Valley Drive in the suburb of Manotick , Ontario . The interchange with Highway 416 is just west of Prince of Wales Drive and south of the Barrhaven development . The section west of the interchange was renamed in the 1990s to Brophy Drive which runs 6.1 km west of Highway 416 to Eagleson Road . Bankfield Road and Brophy Drive formerly ( before recent amalgamation ) formed the border between Nepean , Ontario and Rideau Township , Ontario . Together they are also known as Ottawa Road #8 .
