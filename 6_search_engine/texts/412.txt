Running between Edgware bus station and Colindale , it is operated by Arriva The Shires . # History # Route 303 commenced operating on 1 February 1992 between Edgware bus station and Colindale via Mill Hill . Upon being re-tendered , the route passed from Metroline to Arriva The Shires on 2 September 2006 with Wright Cadet bodied VDL SB120s introduced .
