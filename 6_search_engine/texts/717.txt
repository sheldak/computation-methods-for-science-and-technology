He was appointed to a seat in the North Carolina Senate in 2009 to fill the vacancy caused by the resignation of Sen. David F. Weinstein ( a fellow Democrat ) , and then was elected and re-elected to the Senate in 2010 and 2012 . He chose not to run for another term in 2014 . Walters has represented Senate District 13 , including Columbus , Robeson and Hoke counties .
