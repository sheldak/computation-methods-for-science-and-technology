2034514 The Chilln River is a river located in western Peru . Its waters are produced by the melting of ice in the glaciers of the Andes , and its mouth is located in the Pacific Ocean coast of the Callao Region . Its volume gets higher during the summer months ( December to March ) . The river 's valley is very fertile . It has been inhabited by varying indigenous cultures for more than ten thousand years , as shown by archeological evidence . The 4,000-year-old ruins known as El Paraso are located 40 kilometres north-east of Lima in the Chilln River Valley . A temple at the site is believed to be about 5,000 years old , if the date is confirmed it would be among the oldest sites in the world , comparable to the ancient city of Caral , a coastal city 200 kilometres to the north .
