22225514 Jernej Karniar ( born September 24 , 1982 ) is a Slovenian ski mountaineer . Karniar , member of the ' ' TTSO Jezersko ' ' , was born in Kranj . He started ski mountaineering in 1997 and competed first in the ' ' Memorial Luke in Rada ' ' event 1998 . He has been member of the national selection since 2003 and lives in Jezersko. # Selected results # * 2004 : ** 6th , World Championship relay race ( together with Tone Karniar , iga Karniar and Marko Lihteneker ) * 2006 : ** 9th , World Championship relay race ( together with Nejc Kuhar , Ane enk and Toma Sokli )
