27707514 Karl Auerbach ( 1723&amp;ndash ; c.1786 ) was an Austrian painter . The son of Johann Gottfried Auerbach , he was born at Vienna , and became , under his father 's instruction , a talented painter of portraits and history . The cathedral of St. Stephen in Vienna possesses an altar-piece and a ceilingpainting by him . At Hetzendorf and Innsbruck there are also examples of his art . Karl Auerbach died in Vienna in 1786 ( or 1788 ) .
