34417514 subdivisiontype1 = Province Hrebinky ( ) is an urban-type settlement in the Vasylkiv Raion ( district ) of Kiev Oblast ( province ) in northern Ukraine . Its population is 6,993 as of the 2001 Ukrainian Census . Hrebinky was founded in 1612 as a village , and it retained its village status until it was upgraded to that of an urban-type settlement in 1958 . The M05 highway , connecting the nation 's capital Kiev and the southern city of Odessa , passes through the town .
