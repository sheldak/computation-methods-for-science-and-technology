15390514 William Frederick Varney ( October 1 , 1884 - December 13 , 1960 Rockville Centre , Nassau County , New York ) was an American politician . # Life # He was the son of Rev. F. W. Varney ( d. 1933 ) . He ran on the Prohibition ticket in the United States presidential election , 1928 . He received 20,106 popular votes . In 1934 , he ran on the Law Preservation ticket for Governor of New York , but polled only 20,449 votes and the party lost its automatic ballot access . Besides , he ran several times for other offices , including the New York State Senate , the United States Congress , and Mayor of Rockville Centre , but was always defeated .
